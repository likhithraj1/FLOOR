Fire Safety Instructions

  • Remain calm and do not panic.
  • Follow the marked evacuation routes.
  • Do not use elevators during fire.
  • Proceed to the nearest safe exit.
  • Assist others if it is safe.
  • Do not re-enter the building after evacuation.

Emergency Helpline (Bangalore, India)

  • Fire Emergency: 101
  • National Emergency Helpline: 112
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Bangalore Fire Control Room: 080-22971500
